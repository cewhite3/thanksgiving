Top 5 Thanksgiving Sides

My personal ranking of Thanksgiving sides

Mashed Potatoes

A thanksgiving classic. This fluffy and delicous side is sure to be seen on every family members plate. The buttery goodness mixed with flavors of pepper and rosemary create a delicitable combination no one can resist. A easy choice for number one.

Stuffing

Stuffing is a staple in American homes on the Thanksgiving holiday. Made from breadcrumbs, onion, celery, herbs, and giblets and can be found within the turkey cavity. This savory goodness will spread the scent of Thanksgiving through your home and add to the turkey delight.

Stuffing Recipe

Dinner Rolls

Flakey rolls are perfect for absorbing gravy and butter on your plate. A hot dinner roll coated in butter melts in your mouth while sit at the table. They make a great leftover for turkey sandwiches with cranberry sauce. A staple for the Thanksgiving lineup.

Mac and Cheese

Creamy and cheesy this is a classic comfort dish for Thanksgiving. This family favorite is loved by both children and adults mixing macaroni and a savory cheese sauce. It is a perfect dish for the hoilday.

Green Beans

Southern style green beans consists of bacon and of course, green beans. One of my personal favorites due to its rich savory flavor. Every dinner table should have some sort of green vegetable.
